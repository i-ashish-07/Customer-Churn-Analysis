# 🧠 Business Questions Answered

This project addresses key business questions to identify customer churn patterns and improve retention strategies.

---

### 🔴 1. What is the overall churn rate?

* Measures the **extent of customer loss**
* Helps assess the overall retention performance

---

### 📉 2. Which contract type has the highest churn rate?

* Identifies **high-risk customer segments**
* Helps evaluate the effectiveness of long-term contracts

---

### 🌐 3. Which internet service type is associated with higher churn?

* Analyzes **service performance and customer satisfaction**
* Helps detect issues in premium services

---

### ⏳ 4. How does customer tenure impact churn?

* Identifies **when customers are most likely to leave**
* Helps improve onboarding and early engagement

---

### 💳 5. Which payment method has the highest churn rate?

* Reveals **customer engagement patterns**
* Helps promote automated payment strategies

---

### 🧠 6. What are the primary reasons for customer churn?

* Identifies **root causes (competitor, dissatisfaction, etc.)**
* Helps improve business strategy and service quality

---

### 🔌 7. Which service combinations are associated with higher churn?

* Evaluates **customer usage patterns**
* Helps understand if bundled services provide value

---

### 🛠️ 8. Does premium support reduce churn?

* Measures the **impact of support services**
* Helps justify investment in customer support

---

### 👵 9. Which age group has the highest churn rate?

* Identifies **demographic risk segments**
* Helps in targeted marketing and support strategies

---

### 🌍 10. Which regions/states have the highest churn?

* Highlights **geographical churn patterns**
* Enables region-specific retention strategies

---

## 🎯 Objective of These Questions

These questions help:

* Identify **high-risk customers**
* Understand **why customers churn**
* Provide **data-driven recommendations**
* Improve **customer retention strategies**

---
